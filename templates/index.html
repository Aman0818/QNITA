01 — About

A community for the curious.
Built by students. Backed by industry.

QNITA brings together students who want to do more than read about quantum computing — they want to build with it. We host hands-on Qiskit workshops, mentor first-time researchers, run hackathons, and connect our campus with the global quantum community.

From Bloch spheres to real circuits on IBM hardware, every event is designed to lower the barrier and raise the ceiling.

  • Hands-on Qiskit labs
  • Hackathons
  • Speaker sessions
  • Research mentorship
